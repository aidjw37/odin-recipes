Jay's Jam Recipe

Description

Jay's Famous Jam

Patented in 1989 as "Pump Up the Jam

Ingredients

  • Strawberries
  • Feet
  • Vibes

Steps

  1. Fill two, five-gallon buckets with Strawberries
  2. Put one foot in each five-gallon bucket
  3. Throw on an 80's disco tune to activate the vibes
  4. While feet remain in buckets, dance to the groove until jam is achieved
  5. Enjoy!
  6. Extra step for the vibes: Spread Jay's Jam on a sandwich with Phoebe's PB
