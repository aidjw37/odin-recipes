Phoebe's PB Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 Tub of Peanuts
  • 1 sledgehammer

Steps

  1. Open tub of peanuts
  2. Pickup sledgehammer
  3. Smash peanuts repeatedly until peanuts have buttery texture
  4. Enjoy with spoon or hands!

Description

Odin's cousin legally changed her name to Phoebe to create her brand Phoebe's PB!
