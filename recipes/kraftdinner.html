Kraft Dinner Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 Box of Grandma Krafty's Kraft Dinner
  • 1 full stick of butter

Steps

  1. Bring 4 cups of water to a boil
  2. Add KD Noodles
  3. Let boil for 5 minutes
  4. Turn element off
  5. Strain Water out once noodles have a nice "off-crunch" mouth feel
  6. Add full stick of butter and place pot back on the element (leave the heat off)
  7. Add in that nice highlighter orange powder
  8. Stir rigorously
  9. Enjoy dish
  10. Llight a candle in the bathroom (this step will make sense in 1 hour)

Description

This is Odin's Famous Fat Grandmother's recipe for the one, the only... Kraft Dinner!
